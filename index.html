Welcome!

My name is Brett Glynn.

I am pursuing a BA in Digital Media & Design at the University of Connecticut.

I have a concentration in Game Design & Development and hope to work either in the game industry as a developer, or at a media firm doing graphic design. I have been involved with creating both board games and digital games. I am also currently working on a game in virtual reality.

I have experience coding with the game engines in Unity and Construct 3. I also have experience in coding for web development using HTML5 and CSS3. Outside of class, I have a passion for entertaining people. I am a Twitch Affiliate streamer who plays a multitude of games with the goal to produce quality content and entertainment for my viewers. I have also managed two YouTube accounts in the past, but I prefer the experience of live-streaming.
